PictoQuest

PictoQuest is an adventure-exploration game made in Java. The player can interact with various elements of the environment (switches, ice floor, portals) in order to advance in a ancient temple.

I worked in a 5 perso group during several months. I programmed the game engine, and participated in the game design and artistic direction.
