My profile

I am a 22 year old engineering student at Telecom SudParis, and currently majoring in Video Games and Digital Interactions. This major covers subjects such as Economy of video games, Game design, Virtual and Augmented Realty, and Modeling with Blender.

I've always been a creative person : drawing, writing a bit, building levels in Little Big Planet 2... I have discovered a passion for pixel art three years ago and have been improving my technique as an autodidact ever since. I'm also a long time fan of video games. I'm mostly into narrative games such as Life is Strange or Dragon Age, or games with unique and beautiful artistic direction like Hollow Knight and Horizon : Zero Dawn. Loving both games and creativity naturally lead me towards video game development.
